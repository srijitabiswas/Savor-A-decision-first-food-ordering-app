# 🍽️ Savor — Decision-First Food Ordering Experience

---

## 🚀 Overview

Most food delivery apps optimize for **engagement and scrolling**, not **decision-making**.

Users open the app hungry… and end up overwhelmed.

**Savor** reimagines food ordering as a **decision-first experience** — helping users choose quickly, confidently, and without friction.

---

## 🎯 Problem

Users struggle to decide what to order due to:

* too many choices
* cluttered interfaces
* promotion-heavy feeds
* weak personalization

👉 Result: **decision fatigue + slow ordering**

---

## 💡 Solution

Savor focuses on **clarity over quantity**.

### Core Idea:

> Guide users to the *right choice*, not more choices.

---

## 🔑 Key Features

### 1. 💰 Budget-First Discovery

* Dual slider + quick presets
* Users define price range upfront

---

### 2. 🎯 Curated Results

* “Best Match” (hero recommendation)
* Runner-ups
* Limited, high-quality options

👉 *Less scrolling, faster decisions*

---

### 3. 🍽 Dish Clarity

Each item shows:

* rating ⭐
* delivery time ⏱
* price 💰
* best dish highlight

👉 Builds trust before ordering

---

### 4. 🧠 Decision Support

* “Decide for me” feature
* smart sorting (rating, price)

---

### 5. 🎨 Clean, Premium UI

* minimal layout
* no clutter or aggressive promotions
* calm, focused experience

---

## 🧪 Research Highlights

UX analysis of existing apps revealed:

* excessive options → decision fatigue
* cluttered UI → cognitive overload
* poor structure → slow decision-making

---

* **React Router** — navigation
* **Context API** — state management

* **Netlify** — deployment
